Special Education

Every child deserves a chance to shine — and we make sure they do

favorite Inclusive Learning

Nurturing Specially-Abled Children

At Little Friends, we believe that every child — regardless of ability — is beautifully unique. Our Special Education programme provides a safe, supportive environment where differently-abled children receive individualised attention from trained professionals.

  • Dedicated one-on-one sessions with trained special educators
  • Sensory integration therapy & speech therapy support
  • Individualised Education Plans (IEP) for each child
  • Social inclusion activities with mainstream classmates
  • Regular progress sharing with parents

Abacus

Ancient wisdom for young minds — where beads build brilliant brains

0

Counting Beads, Building Futures

The Abacus is one of the most powerful tools for developing early mathematical intelligence. At Little Friends, children learn mental arithmetic starting from ages as young as 4 — strengthening concentration, memory, and problem-solving skills that last a lifetime.

  • Develops photographic memory through bead visualisation
  • Improves calculation speed by 5–10× over traditional methods
  • Enhances both left and right brain coordination
  • Builds confidence for competitive examinations
  • Age-appropriate levels from beginner to advanced
