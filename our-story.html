Welcome to Little Friends Preschool and Day Care

Nestled in the heart of Newtown, Little Friends Preschool and Day Care has been a beacon of early childhood education since its inception. Our school was founded with a simple yet powerful belief — that every child deserves a nurturing, joyful, and stimulating environment to grow, learn, and thrive.

We blend the warmth of a home with the excellence of modern pedagogy. Our curriculum is aligned to NEP 2020 and international early childhood standards, ensuring that every little learner gets the best start in life. From creative arts to motor skills, from phonics to social development — we nurture the whole child.

🌟 "Where every child is celebrated, every milestone is cherished, and every day is a new adventure in learning."
