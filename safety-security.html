Every Class. Every Child. Always Supervised.

At Little Friends, we maintain a strict 8:1 student-to-teacher ratio in every classroom. Each class is assigned a dedicated, qualified teacher along with 1 trained helper who assists in managing the children, ensuring that no child is ever left unattended.

This low ratio allows our educators to give individualised attention, observe each child's developmental milestones closely, and respond to their unique needs — whether it's a comforting hug, a learning nudge, or a safety intervention.

Dietician-Based Seasonal Meal Chart

Little Friends follows a professionally curated meal chart designed by a certified dietician, updated every season to match the current weather and nutritional demands of growing children. From warm soups in winter to hydrating fruits in summer — every meal is thoughtfully planned.

All meals and snacks are provided by the school. Parents are requested to comply with the prescribed food habits and avoid sending outside food, so that every child benefits from a balanced, hygienic, and age-appropriate diet that supports their physical and cognitive growth.
