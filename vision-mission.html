What We Stand For

Guiding values that shape every child's journey at Little Friends

visibility

Our Vision

To create a nurturing, safe, and stimulating environment where every child can thrive — fostering a lifelong love for learning and exploration while nurturing social, emotional, cognitive, and physical growth. We envision a holistic early childhood education that honours the uniqueness of each child and lays the foundation for a bright, confident future.

rocket_launch

Our Mission

To inspire curiosity, kindle creativity, and build resilience in every child through engaging, play-based, hands-on experiences. We partner with families to create an inclusive community where children develop strong communication skills, emotional intelligence, physical well-being, and the independence to make confident choices — empowering them to succeed in their educational journey and beyond.

Our Core Values

child_care

Child-Centred Learning

Every child is unique with their own interests, strengths, and pace. Our programmes honour individual differences and promote a love for learning through play and exploration.

shield

Safe & Nurturing Environment

Safety is our top priority. We provide a secure, welcoming space where children feel loved, respected, and valued — with caregivers trained to offer emotional support and belonging.

psychology

Holistic Development

We integrate activities that promote cognitive, social, emotional, and physical growth — developing critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills in every learner.

family_restroom

Family Partnership

Parents and families are essential partners. Through open, respectful communication and active involvement, we build a supportive community that enriches every child's experience.

diversity_3

Inclusivity & Diversity

We celebrate diversity and foster an inclusive environment where all children and families feel welcome — teaching respect and appreciation for different cultures and perspectives.

Our Vision for the Future

Little Friends Preschool and Day Care aspires to be a leader in early childhood education — recognised for our unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and inclusivity. We aim to continuously evolve our programmes and facilities, embrace cutting-edge educational practices, and expand our reach to serve more families in our community. Our ultimate goal is to provide a strong foundation for every child, empowering them to flourish in their educational journey and beyond.
