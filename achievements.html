Women's Day Honour

A moment of pride etched in gold

✨ ✨ ✨ ✨
workspace_premium Women's Day 2025

Honoured by Sri Chaitanya Techno School

On the occasion of International Women's Day, Little Friends Preschool and Day Care was honoured with a prestigious trophy by Sri Chaitanya Techno School — recognising the remarkable contribution of our founding team in shaping early childhood education in Newtown.

This accolade is a testament to the tireless dedication of our women-led leadership, whose vision has transformed Little Friends from a small daycare into a beacon of nurturing, innovation, and love for hundreds of little learners and their families.

format_quote

"Behind every confident child walks a strong woman who believed in their potential."
This trophy belongs to every mother, teacher, and caregiver who makes Little Friends a home away from home.
